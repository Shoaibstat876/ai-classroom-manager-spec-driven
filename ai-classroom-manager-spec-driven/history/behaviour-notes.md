## Example 1 — Inattention

# Behaviour Note

**Behaviour Type:** Inattention  
**Date:** 2025-12-01  

---

## Incident Summary
- The student was not maintaining focus during the science lesson.

---

## Behaviour Description
- The student frequently looked away from the board and did not respond when called.

---

## Teacher Response
- The teacher gave a verbal reminder and guided the student to stay attentive.

---

## Recommended Action (Optional)
- Encourage the student to revise the topic at home for better understanding.

---

## Notes
- Improvement was noticed after the reminder.

## Example 2 — Talking During Class

# Behaviour Note

**Behaviour Type:** Disruption  
**Date:** 2025-12-01  

---

## Incident Summary
- The student was talking with a classmate during the explanation of the lesson.

---

## Behaviour Description
- Talking caused disturbance for nearby students and interrupted the flow of the lesson.

---

## Teacher Response
- The teacher instructed both students to maintain silence and continue listening.

---

## Recommended Action (Optional)
- Parents may remind the student to avoid unnecessary talking in class.

---

## Notes
- No further issue observed after the instruction.
