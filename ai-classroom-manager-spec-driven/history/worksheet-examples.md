## Example 1 — Grade 8 Science (Rest & Motion)

# Worksheet — Rest and Motion

**Class:** Grade 8  
**Subject:** Science (Physics — Kinematics)  
**Topic:** Rest and Motion  

---

## Instructions
- Read each question carefully.  
- Answer in the space provided.  

---

## Questions

### MCQs

1. A body is said to be in **rest** when it:  
   - a) Changes its position quickly  
   - b) Does not change its position with respect to surroundings  
   - c) Moves in a circular path  
   - d) Vibrates about a point  

2. A person sitting in a moving bus is in motion with respect to:  
   - a) Other passengers in the bus  
   - b) The seat on which he is sitting  
   - c) A person standing on the road  
   - d) The steering wheel  

### Short Questions

1. Define *rest* in your own words.  
   _Answer: __________________________________

2. Define *motion*.  
   _Answer: __________________________________

3. Give one example of rest and one example of motion from your classroom.  
   _Answer: __________________________________

### Fill in the blanks

1. A body is in ________ when it does not change its position with respect to surroundings.  
2. A body is in ________ when it changes its position with respect to surroundings.  

### True/False

1. A book lying on a table is in rest with respect to the table. (True/False)  
2. A moving car is at rest with respect to a tree on the roadside. (True/False)  

### Match the Following

| Column A          | Column B                    |
|-------------------|----------------------------|
| Fan blades        | Rotatory motion            |
| Child on swing    | To and fro (vibratory)     |
| Car on straight road | Linear motion           |

---

## Answer Key (for teacher use)

### MCQs
1. b  
2. c  

### True/False
1. True  
2. False  

(Short questions may have multiple correct wording options.)

## Example 2 — Grade 5 Science (Water Cycle)

# Worksheet — The Water Cycle

**Class:** Grade 5  
**Subject:** Science  
**Topic:** Water Cycle  

---

## Instructions
- Answer all questions.  
- Use simple sentences.  

---

## Questions

### MCQs

1. The process in which water changes into vapour is called:  
   - a) Condensation  
   - b) Evaporation  
   - c) Precipitation  
   - d) Freezing  

2. Clouds are formed due to:  
   - a) Condensation of water vapour  
   - b) Melting of ice  
   - c) Boiling of water  
   - d) Freezing of water  

### Short Questions

1. Name the three main stages of the water cycle.  
   _Answer: __________________________________

2. What is precipitation?  
   _Answer: __________________________________

### Fill in the blanks

1. ________ is the stage when water vapour cools and forms clouds.  
2. When water falls back to the earth as rain, it is called ________.  

### True/False

1. The water cycle has only one stage. (True/False)  
2. Evaporation happens faster when the Sun is hot. (True/False)  

### Match the Following

| Column A       | Column B         |
|----------------|------------------|
| Evaporation    | Water to vapour  |
| Condensation   | Vapour to clouds |
| Precipitation  | Rainfall         |

---

## Answer Key (for teacher use)

### MCQs
1. b  
2. a  

### True/False
1. False  
2. True
