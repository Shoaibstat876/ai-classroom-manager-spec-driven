# Parent Message Examples — AI Classroom Manager Agent

These examples follow the rules in `specs/parent-message-spec.md`.

---

## Example 1 — Appreciation

**Dear Parent,**

Your child has shown very good participation in class activities this week and is completing work with sincerity. I appreciate their positive attitude towards learning.

**Action Required:**  
- Please encourage your child to continue this good behaviour and regular study habits.

**Thank you,**  
**Class Teacher**

---

## Example 2 — Homework Not Done

**Dear Parent,**

Your child has not completed the assigned homework for the last two days. This can affect their understanding of the current topic.

**Action Required:**  
- Please ensure your child completes homework regularly and brings it to school.

**Thank you,**  
**Class Teacher**

---

## Example 3 — Behaviour Reminder (Mild Issue)

**Dear Parent,**

Your child faced some difficulty in following classroom rules today and was talking during the lesson, which disturbed the class.

**Action Required:**  
- Kindly remind your child to listen carefully in class and avoid unnecessary talking.

**Thank you,**  
**Class Teacher**

---

## Example 4 — Attendance Concern

**Dear Parent,**

Your child has been absent for several days recently. Regular attendance is important for understanding the lessons being taught in class.

**Action Required:**  
- Please try to ensure regular attendance and inform the school in case of any genuine reason for absence.

**Thank you,**  
**Class Teacher**

---

## Example 5 — PTM Reminder

**Dear Parent,**

This is a reminder about the upcoming Parent-Teacher Meeting scheduled for this week. Your presence will help us discuss your child’s progress and future improvement.

**Action Required:**  
- Please attend the Parent-Teacher Meeting as per the given schedule.

**Thank you,**  
**Class Teacher**
